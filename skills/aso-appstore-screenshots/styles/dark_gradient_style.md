# Dark Gradient Style System

Premium dark-mode mobile app marketing screenshot style for DosesPro GLP-1 app.

## Overall Style
- Premium health-tech aesthetic
- Dark background with subtle cyan glow
- Clean, minimal, conversion-focused
- Let the phone and its content be the hero — background supports, never competes

## Color Palette
- Background: near-black `#080808`, dark teal `#0A1A1D`
- Accent: cyan `#10D6E6`
- Text: pure white for headlines

## Background Treatment
- Dark gradient blending near-black to dark teal
- Soft radial cyan glow behind the phone (subtle, not overpowering)
- Black vignette at top and bottom edges
- NO sparkles, NO floating particles, NO abstract icons in background
- Keep it clean and minimal

## Phone Mockup
- iPhone 17 Pro with black frame, centered on canvas
- Full phone visible (no cropping at bottom)
- Screenshot content must fit perfectly inside the screen
- Soft drop shadow behind the device

## Typography
- Line 1 (Verb): SF Pro Display Black (iOS) / Roboto Black (Android)
- Line 2 (Descriptor): SF Pro Text Regular (iOS) / Roboto Regular (Android)
- Verb is heavier/bolder than descriptor for visual hierarchy
- Pure white, all caps, center-aligned
- Subtle text shadow for legibility against glow
- Text stays within center ~70% of canvas (safe zone for aspect ratio cropping)

## Breakout Elements (ONE per screenshot, optional)
- Only ONE UI card from the app screen should break out
- The card floats in front of the phone, extending beyond both left and right bezels
- Frosted glass/glassmorphism border effect
- Drop shadow for depth
- Must look like it came from the app — same colors, same content
- No rotation, no angling — stays at same vertical position as on screen

## Rules
### Do:
- Keep it minimal and clean
- Let the phone and app content be the focus
- Use ONE breakout element maximum
- Dark gradient + glow + vignette — that's it for background

### Avoid:
- Sparkles, particles, glowing dots
- Floating background icons (pie charts, arrows, hearts, syringes)
- Multiple breakout elements
- Bright neon accents that compete with the phone
- Overcrowded compositions
- Redrawing or altering the app screenshot content
